Chinese Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken, pre-cut, 500g
  • Lee-lkum-kee Hoi-sin sauce
  • Green bell-peppers
  • Sitao, 10 strands, chopped julien
  • Onions

How to cook

  1. Simmer chicken into 2 tbps water on a pan
  2. Once the water completely evaporates, add 2 tbps oil and fry
  3. when the chicken is golden brown, add the chopped sitao
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add hoison sauce and oyster sauce and mix well
  6. Serve hot
