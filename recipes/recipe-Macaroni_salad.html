MACARONI SALAD RECIPE

Ingredients

  • Macaroni shells
  • Mayonaise
  • Green bell-peppers
  • Cheese
  • Bacon Spreads

How to cook

  1. Boil the macaroni shell as instructed on the pack
  2. Place the cooked shell on a bowl, set aside for later
  3. Cook the bacon as instructed
  4. Chop the green bell peppers, cheese into small squares
  5. Combine all the ingredients in the bowl and mix well
  6. Chill and serve cold
